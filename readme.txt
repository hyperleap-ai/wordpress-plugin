== Description ==
Enhance your WordPress website with a powerful, customizable AI chatbot. Our plugin enables you to effortlessly add a chatbot by simply entering your unique Chatbot ID and selecting where it should appear on your site.  

**Key Features:**  
- **Customizable Branding:** Choose from various chatbot styles or create a fully branded version using your logo, colors, and guidelines.  
- **Pre-Configured Roles:** Select from roles like Lead Generation Specialist, Product Specialist, Support Specialist, or customize the chatbot for your unique use case.  
- **Quick Integration:** Add the chatbot to any page with a single line—compatible with WordPress, custom-built sites, and popular builders like Webflow and Squarespace.  
- **Lead Generation Ready:** Deploy within 15 minutes and start collecting qualified leads, enhancing customer engagement and driving sales growth.  

With 24/7 natural language interactions, reduce operational costs while providing instant responses to your customers. Get started today and make AI-driven customer engagement effortless.

== Installation ==
**= Admin Installer via Search =**  
1. Go to the **Plugins > Add New** screen in your WordPress dashboard.  
2. Search for **\"AI Chatbots\"**.  
3. Click **\"Install Now\"** and then **\"Activate\"** the plugin.  
4. Navigate to the **\"Chatbots\"** menu to configure your settings.  

**= Admin Installer via ZIP =**  
1. Go to the **Plugins > Add New** screen and click **\"Upload Plugin\"**.  
2. Click **\"Choose File\"** and select the plugin ZIP file from your computer.  
3. Click **\"Install Now\"**, then activate the plugin.  
4. Navigate to the **\"Chatbots\"** menu to configure your settings.  

**= Manual Installation =**  
1. Upload the **AI Chatbots** folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.  
2. In your WordPress dashboard, go to **Plugins > Installed Plugins** and activate **AI Chatbots**.  
3. Navigate to the **\"Chatbots\"** menu to configure your settings.  

---

That\'s it! Now, simply enter your **Chatbot ID**, configure  settings, and choose the pages where the chatbot should appear. Your chatbot will be live in minutes.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How do I get a chatbot ID and private key? =

Visit https://studio.hyperleapai.com to create an account and generate your chatbot credentials.

= Can I use multiple chatbots on different pages? =
Yes! You can configure multiple chatbots and specify which pages they should appear on. However, please note that only one chatbot can be active at a time. This allows you to manage your chatbots effectively while ensuring that your website maintains a streamlined user experience.

= Is my private key stored securely? =
Yes, we follow WordPress security best practices for storing sensitive data.
